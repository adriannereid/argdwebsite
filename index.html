About

Gardens shaped by
place and people

Adrianne is a Wellington-based garden designer with a passion for creating outdoor spaces that feel calm, considered, and rooted in their surroundings.

Every project begins with listening — to you, and to the land. From compact urban courtyards to expansive residential gardens across Wellington and New Zealand, each design is shaped by the site's unique character and the way you want to live.

Services

What I offer

Each service is tailored to where you are in your garden journey — whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refresh an existing space across Wellington and the wider region.

Full Garden Design

Complete design from site analysis through to detailed planting plans and construction documentation.

Planting Design

Curated planting schemes that balance seasonal interest, low maintenance, and a cohesive aesthetic.

Concept Consultation

A focused session to explore possibilities and give you a clear direction to move forward with confidence.

Hard Landscaping Design

Design of terraces, pathways, and structural elements — working with trusted contractors to bring it to life.

Garden Review

A professional assessment of an existing garden with practical recommendations you can act on right away.

Project Management

On-site coordination to ensure your design is executed to the highest standard, exactly as envisioned.
